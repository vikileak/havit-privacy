Privacy Policy

HavIt: Personal Inventory  ·  Last updated April 21, 2026

📦 What we collect

HavIt stores only the data you enter: inventory items, shopping lists, and your account email address. Nothing is collected passively or in the background.

☁️ Where it's stored

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), protected by your sign-in credentials. It is accessible only to your account and is encrypted in transit and at rest.

🚫 What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising profiling.
  • We do not collect analytics or crash reports beyond what Firebase provides.

🔑 Sign-in providers

HavIt supports Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google. These providers only share your name and email address with us. We do not receive your password.

🗑️ Deleting your data

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen inside the app. Deletion is permanent and immediate.

👶 Children

HavIt is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
